NATO Medical Evacuation Stretcher

Modular stretcher paired with a GTM narrative for NATO medical procurement so the value of faster evacuations was visible for medics, strategists, and procurement leads.

Role · Research, positioning, enablement

2024–2025 · Defense · Medical Innovations

Problem

NATO needed a stretcher that could flex across terrains, evacuate faster, and be purchased quickly.

Approach

Led interviews with 50+ medics, distilled JTBD, built personas, and translated insights into positioning, messaging, and launch enablement for the procurement teams.

Outcome

Evacuation times dropped by 30 minutes, procurement aligned on the value story, and the product narrative accelerated deployment across eight units.
